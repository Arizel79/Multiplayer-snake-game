How to Play

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your snake
  • Space or Z - Speed boost
  • T - Open chat
  • TAB - Show player list
  • ESC - Close chat/player list

Game Rules:

  • Eat food to grow longer
  • Avoid colliding with other snakes
  • Don't hit the walls
  • Long snakes cat move fast

Chat Commands:

  • .clear - Clear chat
  • .quit - Return to menu
  • /kill - Suicide
